MySchoolSask Enhancement Suite

Session Timeouts

This extension can override the pre-programmed login session timeout, so that your session doesn't get "timed out" as often.

Do not adjust session timeouts (More secure, but more logging in)
Adjust session timeout to the following:

30 Minutes (MSS Default)
1 Hour
2 Hours
4 Hours
6 Hours

Disable session timeout completely. (The login session will end when the browser closes)

Note: Login sessions are terminated when you close your browser, regardless of any setting currently offered by this browser extension.

Year of Graduation (YOG)

YOG on student registration wizards

This extension can insert a grade selector drop-down box into student registration wizards, so you can simply choose a grade, and let it figure out the YOG and enter it in automatically.

Show a grade selector on student registration wizards
Also hide the "Year of Graduation" fields from registration wizards (only show the grade selector).

Checkbox multi-select with shift+click

This extension can enable checkbox multi-select. Check one checkbox in a list, then shift+click another checkbox, and all checkboxes between the two will also get checked.

Enable checkbox multi-select with shift+click
