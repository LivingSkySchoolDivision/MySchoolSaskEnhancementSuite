About this extension

This browser extension adds usability enhancements to the MySchoolSask web application.

  • This extension and it's authors are not directly affiliated with MySchoolSask.
  • This extension is not endorsed or approved by MySchoolSask.
  • This extension cannot give you access to any data or features of MySchoolSask that you wouldn't normally have.
  • This extension is open source
  • This extension does not collect or track anything from you or your web browser.

Login Session Timeouts

This extension can override the pre-programmed login session timeout, so that your session doesn't get "timed out" as often.

Warning: Login session timeouts are there to protect your MySchoolSask account from unauthorized use. Disabling session timeouts will make your MySchoolSask account less secure.

Always lock your computer when you are not using it, so that others can't use your account. To quickly lock your computer, press WINDOWS KEY + L.


Do not adjust login session timeouts
Adjust login session timeout to the following:

1 Hour
2 Hours
4 Hours
6 Hours

Keep my login session alive indefinitely

Note: Login sessions are terminated when you close your browser, regardless of any setting currently offered by this browser extension.

Checkbox multi-select with shift+click

This extension can enable checkbox multi-select. Check one checkbox in a list, then shift+click another checkbox, and all checkboxes between the two will also get checked.
