MySchoolSask Enhancement Suite

Session Timeouts

This addon can override the pre-programmed login session timeout, so that your session doesn't get "timed out" as often.

Do not adjust session timeouts (More secure, but more logging in)
Adjust session timout to the following:

30 Minutes (MSS Default)
1 Hour
2 Hours
4 Hours
6 Hours

Disable session timeout completely. (The login session will end when the browser closes)

Note: Login sessions are terminated when you close your browser, regardless of any setting currently offered by this browser extension.

Problems or concerns?

Visit this addon's GitHub repository to report issues or view source code:
https://github.com/LivingSkySchoolDivision/MySchoolSaskEnhancementSuite

This extension does not collect any data from you or your web browser.
